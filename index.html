Interests

One of my primary interests right now is the intersection of linguistics and programming. (I already warned you, I am a nerd) Recently I sat with one of my university's linguistics professors, and together we observed that often CS is applied to the field of linguistics, but not the other way around. As an example of what this might mean, in language you can have easy to understand and hard to understand sentences which are both gramatically correct, and we have frameworks for understanding the structure of a well constructed sentence. I wonder if we have as well defined frameworks for understanding the structure of a well constructed code block.
